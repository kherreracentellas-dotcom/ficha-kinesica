# Product Specification: Ficha Kinésica Integral

## Overview
A digital tool for kinesiology professionals to perform cardiorespiratory assessments in clinical environments.

## Target Users
- Kinesiologists in hospitals, clinics, or private practice.
- Students of Kinesiology.

## Key Features
1. **Clinical Assessment Form:** Comprehensive fields for patient data, vital signs, physical examination, and functional tests (GOLD, TM6M).
2. **Auto-Calculations:** Real-time calculation of BMI, PAM, and test scores to reduce human error.
3. **Multi-User Local Profiles:** Professional login simulation to separate data between different kinesiologists using the same device.
4. **Professional PDF Export:** High-quality document generation for clinical records.
5. **Digital Signatures:** Capture of kinesiologist and patient signatures directly on the device.
6. **PWA Support:** Full offline capability for use in areas with poor connectivity.

## Success Criteria
- The app loads in under 1 second.
- PDF reports match official clinical templates.
- Data is persistent even if the browser is closed.
